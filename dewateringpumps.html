Dewatering Pumps

Dewatering pumps are centrifugal pumps installed in a project site that is situated below the groundwater level, so that pump can pump the water from source and pump it to the next level. Sovereign pumps are portable type and completely submerged in water. An example is in underground tunnelling in which water penetrating the audits is pumped up to the surface through dewatering pumps.

Advantages of Dewatering Pumps

  • Pumps are portable.

  • Not required Installation except delivery hose pipe connection.

  • Light weight.

  • Parts are readily available in the market.

  • Environment friendly, No noise and carbon emissions

Application where Dewatering Pumps are used?

  • Tunneling dewatering.

  • Bypass dewatering.

  • Steel plants.

  • Flood control.

  • Wellpoint Dewatering.

  • Deep well Dewatering.

  • Pile Dewatering for bridge.
