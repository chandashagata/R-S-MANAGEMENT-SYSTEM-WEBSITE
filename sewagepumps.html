Sewage Pumps

Sovereign sewage Pumps are centrifugal pumps installed in STP/ ETP and Municipal for transferring raw untreated sewage and treated sewage. Sovereign pumps work under 6-10 Ph value. The Pumps are used in every city manhole to collect human waste and other aggregates which helps them to transfer that sludge to main drainage line. These pumps have big range to handle suspend solid and to transfer sludge.

Advantages of Sovereign Dewatering Pumps

  • No Corrosive Body.

  • Bigger sludge and suspended solid handling capacity.

  • Low maintenance.

  • Clogging free pumps.

  • Maximum discharge capacity.

Application where Sovereign Dewatering Pumps are used?

  • ETP Plants.

  • STP Plants.

  • Municipal Waste.

  • Building ETP.

  • Industrial Waste.

  • Food Processing Parks.
