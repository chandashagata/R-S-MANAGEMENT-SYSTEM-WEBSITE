The "HET" make "2PED" Series pump are desiged in single stage, vertical submerisble by A.C. siquirrel cage induction "F" class motor . These pump are equipped with double mechanical seal and excellence performance capacitiy with compact light weight design.

The "HET" make “2PED” series is designed to meet dewatering demands during floods, stormwater, sewer clogging, and drainage overflow. These pumps are heavy-duty industrial strength but still light and highly portable with ease in the handle. These pumps are ideal for pumping water with a solid content up to 35mm. this pump comes with plug and play concept with either internal or external capacitor provision.
